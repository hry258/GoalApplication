What is the S.M.A.R.T. framework?

A S.M.A.R.T. Framework is an acronym for Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Relevant, and Time-bound. It provides the framework for setting clear and attainable goals in project management.

The SMART Framework also helps you achieve and focus on your goals. The elements in the framework work together to create a goal that is carefully and thoughtfully planned out, executable and trackable.
